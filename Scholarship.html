Scholarship

The national scholarship exam question paper will consist of Multiple Choice Questions (MCQs). It is an online exam conducted for students from class 5 to undergraduate degrees. The national scholarship exam question papers are available in English and regional languages. The organisers design the national scholarship exam question papers to test the students' reasoning and decision-making abilities. Therefore, the students are advised to read the questions thoroughly before answering them.

Besides recognising and nurturing young talents, the National Scholarship Exam (NSE) also helps students identify and work on their strengths and weaknesses. NSE serves as an opportunity for the applicants to test their capabilities in handling new education challenges. The national scholarship exam will not have negative markings. School students from the SSC, ICSE or CBSE board can apply to the NSE exam. Similarly, the national scholarship exam for college students is available irrespective of their selected disciplines and universities.
