About Us

To stay confident and get away from fear of mathematics, there is a need for every student to learn mathematics techniques for personal and academic growth. Saraswati Careers Academy acts as a facilitator, tutor and mentor for the students and aspirants who always look forward to enhance their skills. We constantly update our curriculum to meet global competitive requirements and constantly changing needs in the competitive world.

Who we are:

Saraswati Careers Academy founded in 2020 has grown to become global in Vedic Maths training and consulting. We have an experience of 100+ students participating in the 10+ online batches of Vedic Maths and Abacus from India, Australia and United Kingdom. It was successful journey with all the students and would like to connect with most of the maths passionate.
